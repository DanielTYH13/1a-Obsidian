- Thm: Given the function f(x) is differentiable on interval I, then
	- If $f'(x) > 0$ over I, f(x) is increasing on I.
	- If $f'(x) < 0$ over I, f(x) is decreasing on I.
	- If $f'(x) = 0$ over I, f(x) is constant on I.
